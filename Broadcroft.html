Broadcroft House B&B

Bed and Breakfast mobile application tool

Broadcroft House is a Bed and breakfast in a small village called Horton In Ribblesdale. In my final year at Newcastle University I worked on a project titled "How technology impacts hotel customer satisaction" and used Broadcroft House as a way to gather data and test the hypothesis by creating a mobile application based from primary research coducted using past and present guests at the B&B. This dissertaion was undertaken during the Covid-19 pandemic making data collection and testing of the prototype mobile application impossible as the Bed and Breakfast closed to the public. Due to this the dissertaion was not finished as expected and other methods for qaulatative data collection had to be utilised to gather user feedback. In addition to this the applicaion could not be fully incorporated into the business. As explained in the dissertation in the future this mobile application will be polished up and fully incorporated into Broadcroft House Bed and Breakfast.

FontBoard

Utilising the moodboard, a couple of simple mock-ups fontboards were created for the project. These simple mock-ups show a combination of two fonts. The first one is Roboto, the default font used in android applications. The second was a font similar to that used in the website called Segoe. The fonts were then compared to over some simple colour combinations, one being a green environment theme, one a sky-blue theme and the final and most likely one to be used was the theme for the website again. A combination of font colours was not used as black will be the most effective for these themes as they are all light.
